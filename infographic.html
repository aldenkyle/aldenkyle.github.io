Design and Analysis: Longform Infographic

In my Graphic Design in Cartography course in spring 2022 we were asked to design a long form infographic to tell a compelling and data-driven visual story. I chose to produce a graphic related to my work at USAID's Bureau for Resilience and Food Security which details a framework for identifying the best places to invest in agriculture-led economic development based on localities level of need and level of opportunity.
